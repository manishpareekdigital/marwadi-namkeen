Cancellation & Refund Policy

चूंकि हमारे उत्पाद खाद्य पदार्थ (Perishable food items) हैं, इसलिए एक बार डिलीवरी पूरी होने के बाद हम रिटर्न या रिफंड की पेशकश नहीं करते हैं।

1. Damaged / Spoiled Shipments: If you receive a damaged package or a packet with split vacuum seals, please notify us within **24 hours** of delivery with clear photographs at pareekgautam152@gmail.com. We will ship a replacement free of charge.

2. Order Cancellations: Orders can only be cancelled within **2 hours** of placement or before dispatch (whichever is earlier). Once the kitchen prepares and packs the fresh batch, cancellations cannot be processed.

3. Refund Disbursals: Approved cancellations will trigger automatic refunds to the original payment source via Razorpay, which typically reflects in your UPI wallet or bank account within **5-7 business days**.
